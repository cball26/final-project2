Hello! I'm Connor Ball

I am a fourth year at the University of California, San Diego. I am from the bay area which is where I have done most of my activities that pertain to the medical field. These opportunities have allowed me to gain awareness on the path that lies ahead.

I have always found value in giving back. For me, becoming a doctor allows me to change lives and futures that are fulfilled. When I was ten, I had a bad shoulder injury from baseball. I was not sure if I was going to be able to keep playing the sport that I loved. This all changed when I went to an orthopedic surgeon who operated on it and got me back on the field. This experience was life changing as the baseball diamond was an integral part of my childhood where I found most of my closest friends.

When looking at this experience, I was only one life, one patient if you will, that this doctor saw on his daily routine. To know that I could have that ability in changing lives, is something that I would not pass up.
